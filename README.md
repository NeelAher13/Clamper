## 🔍 What is Clamper?

Clamper is a simple application designed to keep your menu bar steady and easy to access. If your menu bar moves or disappears while you work, Clamper "clamps" it in place. This helps you access your important tools and settings without interruption.

Clamper runs quietly in the background. Once set up, it takes care of your menu bar automatically. You don’t need technical skills to use it.

## ⚙️ How Clamper Works

Clamper works by monitoring your menu bar and stopping it from moving or shrinking when you don’t want it to. This is useful if your menu bar tends to collapse or hide when you switch between programs or adjust your screen.

The app runs in the background without disturbing your workflow. It allows you to keep your tools and options visible and ready to click.

## 🔧 Using and Configuring Clamper

Once installed, Clamper is easy to use. It does not require daily interaction. However, there are a few settings you may want to check:

- **Start on Boot**: Clamper can start automatically when you turn on your computer. This means your menu bar will always stay in place without the need to open the app manually.

- **Pause Clamping**: If you need your menu bar to move temporarily, you can pause Clamper from its tray icon. Right-click the Clamper icon near your clock and choose “Pause.”

- **Resume Clamping**: To turn the clamping back on, simply right-click the tray icon and select “Resume.”

- **Settings Access**: Advanced settings can be accessed through the tray icon menu. Here you can adjust how often Clamper checks your screen or other preferences.

## 👨‍💻 Troubleshooting

Here are some common issues and how to solve them:

- **Clamper does not start automatically**  
  Make sure you allowed it to launch on startup during installation. Check your system’s startup programs and enable Clamper if disabled.

- **Menu bar still moves or hides**  
  Restart Clamper. Sometimes a reboot of your computer helps reset the settings.

- **Clamper icon missing from tray**  
  Click the arrow near your clock to see hidden icons. If still not visible, restart the app.

- **Installation fails**  
  Confirm your Windows is updated. Run the installer as Administrator by right-clicking and selecting “Run as Administrator.”
